What is user status in Dinero Cash?

The better Dinero Cash knows you, the further you go with Dinero Cash... In accordance with the regulations and for safety reasons, it is essential that you are perfectly identified by Dinero Cash. Dinero Cash has therefore defined 3 statuses, Bronze, Silver and Gold, depending on the level of information Dinero Cash has about you. According to this status, you have a more or less important wallet limit and transaction limit. Your status level is identifiable thanks to the stylized map at the top left of your application.

Bronze status

The Bronze status is the initial status in Dinero Cash. This status is open to any natural person who has charged the application and provided the necessary information for its identification. This status is granted for a very short period of time, just until the Dinero Cash teams check the quality of the information provided. However, this status allows you to use Dinero Cash immediately and make transfers or payments. The amount of the limits is available on the Rates and Conditions page.

Silver status

The Silver status confirms that you have provided correct identification data to Dinero Cash. You are now a user fully recognized by Dinero Cash and that is why Dinero Cash offers you wider conditions and limits of use. The amount of these limits can be viewed on the Rates and Conditions page. Want to go even further with Dinero Cash? Then go to the Gold status....

Gold Status

Gold status is the highest user status in Dinero Cash, giving you access to Dinero Cash with even higher limits (available on the Rates and Conditions page). To access this status, you have two options: either go physically to a bank branch, which will then take the necessary steps, or simply link your bank account to your Dinero Cash application.
