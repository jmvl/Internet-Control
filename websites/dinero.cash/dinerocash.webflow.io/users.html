Pay easily!

Dinero Cash makes paying for anything a breeze! Whether it's a taxi ride, coffee, bills, or shopping - just whip out your phone, open Dinero Cash, scan the QR code, enter your PIN and voila! Payment done! Plus, you'll get a confirmation that the payment went through, so you can feel confident and secure. No more fumbling with cash or cards, just easy, fast and fun payments with Dinero Cash!

Request money!

Get money for a service or borrow from someone with ease using Dinero Cash. Simply create a payment request, turn it into a QR code on your phone and share on social media or show it to the person you want to get funds from.

They scan the code using their phone camera and the funds are instantly transferred to your account. No more complicated transactions, just quick and easy payments with Dinero Cash.

Transfer money to friends and relatives

Want to send money to grandma? No problem! Just get a token from the nearest Dinero Cash agent and send the token number to grandma. The nearest agent in her area will turn the token into cash. Easy and fast!

Get paid

Get paid on time, every time with Dinero Cash! Receive your salary directly to your Dinero Cash account, no need to visit the bank. Plus, get real-time notifications when your money comes in. Check your balance on your phone and start paying for the things you love right away.

Absolute safety of your clients’ finances

At Dinero Cash, we understand the importance of security and the peace of mind that comes with keeping your money safe. Our platform is designed with advanced security measures to protect your finances from theft. Unlike carrying cash, even if someone were to steal your phone, they would not be able to access your money. You have the power to restore full control over your finances at any time.
